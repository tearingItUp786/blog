Amor fati is a Latin phrase that may be translated as "love of fate" or "love of one's fate". This stoic concept
mirrors similar philosophies such as `Taqdeer` in Arabic, loosley translated as the absolute decree of the Divine or
`Chardi Kala` in Sikhism.
